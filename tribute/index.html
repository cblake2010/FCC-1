Here's some interesting facts about Peter Griffin

  • His full name is Peter Löwenbräu Griffin.
  • He is of Irish, Mexican descent.
  • He is married to Lois, the daughter of the wealthy Pewterschmidt family.
  • He has three children; Meg, Chris and Stewie.
  • He is the biological father of Bertram, Stewie’s frenemie.
  • Peter had a son previously with Lois. Peter Griffin Jr died after Peter shook him too much.
  • His ‘best buddy’ is an adopted dog, Brian.
  • Seth Macfarlane, the creator of Family Guy voices three Griffin family members; Peter, Brian and Stewie.
  • The Griffins live on Spooner Street, in Quahog, Rhode Island.
  • His best friends, and neighbours are; sex manic Glen Quagmire, paraplegic police officer Joe Swanson and African-American Cleveland Brown.
  • He was once best friends with Hollywood actors, James Woods.
  • His paternal father is Irishman, Mickey McFinnigan, the town drunk.
  • Francis Griffin (Peter’s step-father) is a devout Catholic and often commented on Peter’s lifestyle.
  • Peter is clinically obese, weighing in at 270 lbs.
  • Proud of his weight, Peter created the coalition for fat men in Fat Guy Strangler; he is nearly killed by Lois’ long lost brother.
  • His intelligence is so low, he is classed as mentally retarded.
  • Peter’s had many jobs including; towel boy, toy maker, fisherman, office lackey and starting centre of the New England Patriots football team.
  • He has gone undercover at James Woods high as ‘Lando Griffin’ to stop a toad licking epidemic. The episode spawns the song ‘Give Up the Toad Now’; an homage to Grease.
  • He wrote a number of erotic novels.
  • Peter is a fan of the band KISS and is incredibly proud when Lois reveals she once slept with Gene Simmons.
  • He loves television. In the episode If I’m Lying, I’m Dying he defrauded a television company to get the show ‘Gumbel 2 Gumbel’ back.
  • Peter broadcast his own television station after the FCC ban; he called it PTV.
  • When Lois conceded defeated over PTV, Peter unfurled a banner, claiming he was right. He had placed it there fifteen years ago and the clown inside it had died.
  • In Family Guy‘s retelling of Star Wars, Peter takes on the character of Han Solo.
  • His favourite song is the Trashmen’s ‘Surfin’ Bird’.
  • Peter toured the Pawtucket Brewery with Brian, in an homage to Charlie and the Chocolate Factory.
  • He has the least respect for his daughter Meg, coining the catchphrase, ‘Shut up Meg.’
  • During a father-son competition, Peter had penis envy over his son Chris.
  • He often fights Ernie the Giant Chicken.
  • He has out-farted filmmaker, author and political commentator, Michael Moore.
